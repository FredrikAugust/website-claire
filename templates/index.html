Saudade

Saudade is an interdisciplinary exhibition exploring longing tied to love rather than loss. Through film, painting, sound, and spatial installation, it translates the feeling of saudade into a sensory experience. Created by Canadian artist Claire Foody and Danish painter Ida Brieghel, the work reflects a shared experience of living away from home. Drawing inspiration from the fairy tales and travels of H.C. Andersen, the project bridges literary heritage with contemporary art. The central dance film, Wish You Were Here, filmed in the fields of Bellinge, Odense, is accompanied by an original score by Emil Mark. The installation structures — domestic elements such as a sofa, table, wardrobe, and chandelier — embed screens and reworked materials from the film, while the paintings echo its themes of longing and connection, acting as points of pause within the space. Saudade reminds audiences that even in loneliness, one can find community. Man kan godt savne og høre til på samme tid.
